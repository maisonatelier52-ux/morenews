Get in Touch with More News

More News is an independent UK newsroom. We welcome tips, corrections, and communication from readers, journalists, organisations, and individuals responding to our coverage. Please use the address that best fits your enquiry.

Editorial & General Enquiries

Questions about our reporting, ideas for coverage, or matters relating to editorial decisions and published articles.

Confidential News Tips

If you have information you believe warrants investigation or public reporting, you can contact us securely. We handle all tips with care and discretion.

Corrections

If you believe we have made a factual error, please let us know promptly. Include the article link and the specific claim you believe is wrong so we can review it quickly.

Media & Press Enquiries

For journalists, researchers, or organisations seeking commentary, collaboration, or information about the newsroom.

Contact Addresses

  • Editorial: editorial@morenews.org
  • Tips: tips@morenews.org
  • Corrections: corrections@morenews.org
  • Rights, permissions & formal notices: editorial@morenews.org

When contacting us about a published article, please include the article URL and the specific issue you are raising. This helps us route your request to the right person without delay.
